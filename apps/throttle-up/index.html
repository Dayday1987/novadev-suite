Game Instructions

Tap the screen for a quick boost or hold to gradually lift the front wheel and increase speed.

Keep the front tire off the ground to continue the game. If it touches or you hit an obstacle, game over.

Use A/D keys or swipe left/right to change lanes and collect coins.

Your score is based on distance traveled.
